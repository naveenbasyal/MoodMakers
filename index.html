Little Story

Mood Makers is a one stop shop where we can assist you with all professional bartending consultancy services! By professional we mean remembering little things, such as pre-chilled glass for every “Martini” & which drink gets a sugared rim - "Daiquiri".

What happens when you entertain your guest’s at private parties, weddings, events, product launches? Not only you are expected to have a fully stocked bar, but those baby pearl onions that better be ready, and you may hear reports that the drapes are on fire Panicked! We sought just the solution for you at ‘Bar Solution’s a firm having great quality bartending services that today’s discerning hosts have come to expect.

The founders being Raj Wadhwa recognized that while there are a few on premise/party/event bartending services in India, these services fell far short of the expectations. The 8 years of high-volume and fine dining bartending experience have today embarked detailed services like bar designing, menu designing, training & recruitment for various segments of the hospitality industry thus filling a gap they perceived in the professional bartending industry. Mood Makers is a professionally organised entity comprising of a team of trained and experienced bartenders which is headed by Raj Wadhwa who is designated today as the Head Client Servicing & Operations. He brings in 8 years of experience in enormous upscale bars, where he has handled kaleidoscope of liquor bottles.
