Welcome To Mood Makers Restaurant

Little Story

MM-"Mood Makers" is a company well known for creating unforgettable celebrations that exude a fresh and modern perspective. Our bartending expertise ranges from upscale society weddings at exclusive destinations to cutting-edge artistic events for music luminaries, concerts, private parties and celebrities in the film industry and corporate world.


Organized, creative & enthusiastic, the team always brings unwavering enthusiasm to every one of their clients.


While planning a party or wedding, you might assume your catering company will supply the bartending staff. Many don't.


When you need to hire wedding or party bartenders, you'll find "MOOD MAKERS" to be an invaluable resource. Surely you not only want someone who knows how to mix drinks, but also a person who is an expert and understands how to greet and serve your guests with professionalism.


Dream it - live it - love it! "MOOD MAKERS" brings you all the expertise, enthusiasm, and aesthetic excellence you'll ever need to turn your ideas and inspirations into reality. "MOOD MAKERS" has earned to be on the recommendation list of the most prestigious private clients and hotels across India.


We are experts at creating an environment that brings the party alive while serving flawless variety of drinks such as smoked shots, flaming shots, classic Or signature cocktails and many such others.


Bartender checklists are provided for you and your server that discuss details that shouldn't be overlooked. Theme based props, attire; Designer menu will be exclusively created to suit your event.


We have extremely well trained versatile professional male/ female bartenders, mixologists, liquid chefs, flair tenders. Celebrity bartenders who would love to entertain at your next event and make it a most memorable one.
